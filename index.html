Don't shop, adopt!

Welcome to our cat adoption platform, where users can create ads to find loving new homes for their feline friends. We want to remind all our users that the cats available on this site are not for sale. All adoption fees are intended to cover veterinary care and other needs of the animals.

Our values:

  • Love and care: Every cat deserves a loving and caring home. Ensure that your ads respect this commitment.
  • No profit: Ads for selling cats are strictly prohibited on our platform. Any attempt to sell will result in the ad being removed and the user being banned.
  • Lifelong commitment: By creating an ad, you commit to finding a safe and loving home for your cat. Adopting a cat is a lifelong commitment.

Report abuse:

We rely on our community to maintain the safety and well-being of the animals on our site. If you witness or suspect abuse, including attempts to sell or mistreat animals, please inform us immediately.
